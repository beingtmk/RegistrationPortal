Feature

Description

Duration of Exam

2 hours (120 minutes)

Subjects

KKSO question paper will consist of 5 Subjects:

  • Mathematics(20 Questions)

  • Physics(20 Questions)

  • Chemistry(20 Questions)

  • Biology(20 Questions)

  • 4 Marks for every correct answer

  • No Negative Marking.

KKSO Eligibility Criteria

The students who are promoted to classes 8,9,10,11 in April 2018 can participate in KKSO.
